Penne ala Vodka

Description:

This penne alla vodka recipe is quick, easy, and incredibly delicious. It's the perfect impressive weeknight dinner!

Ingredients:

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this penne alla vodka recipe:

  • Tomato products: This pasta alla vodka calls for both a can of whole peeled tomatoes and a couple tablespoons of tomato paste.
  • Pancetta and oil: Cook pancetta in olive oil.
  • Onion: You'll need ½ cup of finely diced onion.
  • Seasonings and herbs: This flavorful penne alla vodka is seasoned with garlic, crushed red pepper, salt, pepper, fresh parsley, and fresh basil.
  • Vodka: Of course, you'll need vodka. use whatever brand you have on hand.
  • Pasta: Opt for penne or rigatoni.
  • Cream: Heavy cream is the key to a rich, decadent vodka sauce.
  • Cheese: Make sure to use freshly grated Parmesan cheese instead of the pre-shredded stuff.

Steps:

Here are the brief steps to make Penne alla Vodka:

  1. In a blender or food processor, puree the can of whole peeled tomatoes until smooth. Set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the pancetta (or bacon) and cook until crispy. Remove the pancetta from the pan and set aside on a paper towel-lined plate.
  3. In the same pan with the pancetta drippings, add the finely diced onion and sauté until translucent and softened.
  4. Add the minced garlic, crushed red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to the pan. Sauté for another minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.
  5. Pour in the tomato puree and tomato paste. Stir well to combine with the onion and garlic mixture.
  6. Add the torn basil leaves and a handful of chopped parsley to the sauce. Stir and let it simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the penne or rigatoni pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  8. Back to the sauce, pour in the vodka and stir. Allow the sauce to simmer for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the alcohol to cook off.
  9. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream. Simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  10. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the sauce. Toss the pasta in the sauce until well coated.
  11. Serve the Penne alla Vodka in individual bowls or plates. Top with the crispy pancetta and freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
